Report a smell

Use this service to report large-scale, unpleasant smells in England. This includes smells from:

  • rubbish tips, landfill or other waste management sites
  • industrial processes
  • agriculture

Before you start

You'll need:

  • the location where the smell has caused you a problem
  • the dates and approximate times when you noticed the smell

If you've reported the smell before then you can report it again, if it has happened again.

Do not use this service to report nuisance smells from your neighbours, domestic rubbish or small local businesses, such as restaurants. Report this to your local council's environmental health department.

Other environmental problems

How you report other problems affecting the environment depends on the type of problem it is and where it's happening. You may have to report it to:

  • the Environment Agency
  • your local council
  • your utilities company
