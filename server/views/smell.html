Report a smell

Use this service to report unpleasant smells from industry and businesses in England.

This includes smells from:

  • rubbish tips
  • recycling centres
  • other waste management activities

Before you start

You'll need to know:

  • where the smell is coming from, for example the name of the site or business responsible
  • where you were when you noticed the smell
  • when the smell started
Do not use this service to report nuisance smells from your neighbours, domestic rubbish or small local businesses, such as restaurants. Report these to your local council's environmental health department.

Other environmental problems

How you report other problems affecting the environment depends on what it is and where it's happening. You may have to report it to:

  • the Environment Agency
  • your local council
  • your utilities company
