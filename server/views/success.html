What happens next

The Environment Agency will now:

  • look into the incident
  • check for any similar reports in the area
  • decide how to deal with the incident, which may include sending people to investigate
  • pass your report to other organisations if needed
  • contact you only if needed, if you have left your contact details

You can see your issue and the current stage of the Environment Agency response online.
