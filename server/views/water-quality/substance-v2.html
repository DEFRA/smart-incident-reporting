What do you think is in the water?

This can help us locate where the pollution may be coming from and decide how to deal with it.
See what sewage in water looks like

What to look for

Sewage turns the water brown, dark and murky.

See what oil or petrol in water looks like

What to look for

Oil or petrol usually has a rainbow film on the surface of the water, may also smell strongly.

See what agricultural waste looks like

What to look for

Agricultural waste will leave brown, dark or green smears, may be a light rainbow film and algae.

See what rubbish or refuse looks like

What to look for

Rubbish or or refuse means you can clearly see waste or dumped objects in the water, for example domestic bin bags.
