Report the smell to your local council

Contact your local council to report a nuisance smell from:

  • neighbouring properties
  • local businesses, such as restaurants
  • uncollected or dumped household waste
  • an unknown source

Check your local council's website - you may be able to report the problem online. Otherwise contact their environmental health team.
